Your voice stays yours.

Microphone audio NEVER leaves your device. Only approved text tool requests (like asking for the weather) may leave, and every single action is recorded in a local, transparent privacy ledger.

No Audio Uploads

Speech-to-text happens entirely on-device. Your raw voice is never sent to the cloud.

Approved Tools Only

Only specific, user-approved text requests (like fetching weather data) can access the internet.

Privacy Ledger

Complete transparency. Review a local log of every action and tool request Trusty makes.

Why Trusty?

See how Trusty compares to existing solutions in the market.

Vs. Big Tech
(Alexa, Google, Siri)

Big tech assistants send your raw voice audio to giant cloud servers to be processed and recorded. Trusty processes all audio on-device. No cloud accounts, no subscriptions, no surveillance.

Vs. Traditional Open Source
(Rhasspy, HA Assist)

Most offline assistants match your voice against rigid, pre-trained intent templates. You have to speak exact phrases. They lack the conversational intelligence of modern AI.

The Trusty Difference

Trusty uses Gemma 4 as the actual orchestrator. It uses reasoning to pick tools, synthesize arguments, and understand natural language. The LLM is the brain of the home, not just a chat layer.

Everything you need, running locally.

Local Gemma 4
